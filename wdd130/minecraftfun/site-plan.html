basics

What is Redstone?-Did you know that Redstone is one of the more advanced elements in Minecraft. You can obtain it either by mining a block of Redstone ore or by acquiring it from mob/dungeon loot. Redstone occurs across certain biomes in lower levels of the terrain. You are more likely to see Redstone ore in caverns in cube-shaped veins. Before you mine it, of course your pickaxes need to be either Iron, Dimond or neitherite; so no gold, stone or wood axes can do the job. Also, when you are looking for it, remember it generates low in the worlds, about 15 and below, and since the 1.18 update and the world now goes down to y=-64, you have a better chance of finding it while mining. You can also find it naturally in the world. 15 lengths of Redstone dust are naturally generated as part of the trap in each jungle pyramid. 5 lengths of Redstone dust can be found in one type of jail cell room in a woodland mansion. In ancient cities, one of the newest updates to the game, multiple pieces of Redstone dust can be found integrated into circuitry. Along with the witch in the game, if you kill her she drops between 0-2 Redstone. Redstone power-Now to understand just how powerful this stuff is, you need to understand that Redstone can be added to almost every different kind of potion you’re brewing to increase it durability from about 3 minutes to 8 minutes. It can also be used to make compasses, clocks dispensers, detector rail, dropper and so much more. This stuff once you understand its power, can do almost anything you want in the game.
